PUMPKIN SLASHER: BLOODBATH

Pumpkin Slasher: Bloodbath is a horror-action game submitted to the 2024 Granola.gg X Rit game jam under the theme: nostalgia. You just got home with the hottest new video game: Pumpkin Slasher Bloodbath. When booting up the cartridge you realize there is something more sinister lying beneath the surface of the game. Switch between first person and in-game perspectives to fix bugs and warn off the evil entity haunting the cartridge.

Team Project Software: Game Maker Studio 2, Paint.NET, Audacity Roles: Main Game Designer, Main Programmer, Artist Unique Systems: Perspective Switching, Minigames

PROCESS & DETAILS

Pumpkin Slasher: Bloodbath was a team project of 3. We began by researching & locking down our theming and gameplay style which we decided to go with an 80s horror styled game where you play as a classic slasher character. We knew that we wanted imagery of CRT tvs and old game consoles. We thought of having the title screen be a first person perspective where you insert the game cartridge to play. Building on that I presented my teammates with the idea of switching between a first person perspective being a core mechanic. I designed three mingames with the theme of nostalgia in mind: fixing tv signal, fixing joystick input, and blowing dust off the cartridge. While our team dynamics were great, I did end up spreading myself too thin acting as the main game designer, main programmer, and an artist. I learned to not let myself take charge in too many areas and make sure to spread the work more evenly throughout my team.

ROLL ARENA

Roll Arena is a two player platform-fighting game submitted for the 2022 GMTK Game Jam under the theme: roll of the dice. Every aspect of the game's setup is up to chance such as character & stage. Two players spike a dice back and forth at each other as it randomly spins to one of its six sides. If you get hit by it the damage you take is determined by the side the dice is on. This was my first game jam and it sparked my interest & joy in participating in these events.

Solo Project 2022 GMTK Game Jam Software: Game Maker Studio 2, Paint.NET, Audacity Roles: Game Designer, Artist, Programmer, Level Designer Unique Systems: Combat System, Movement System

PROJECT DETAILS

To challenge myself, I focused on developing a two player 1v1 game. I developed a system allowing for every combination of gamepad and keyboard input for both players. This allows both on two gamepads, both on one keyboard, and P1 or P2 on keyboard or gamepad. I wanted to create a fun & chaotic experience. To achieve this I designed the dice so that each spike by a player increases the speed of the block slightly. I mixed this idea with the damage system being determined by the randomized rolling of the dice block to both create this sense of choas and fit within the theming of the game jam.

CHANGELING

Changeling VR is a first-person VR mystery where you play as detective Aurelia Walker, a private eye with the power to dream-walk — seeing the memories of anyone she touches.

Team Project Software: Unreal Engine 4, Perforce, ClickUp Roles: Level Designer, Environmental Design Unique Systems: Scripted Subway Sequence

PROJECT DETAILS

For Changeling, I redesigned and implemented the “Mother’s Level” in UE4, creating unique setpieces and concept sketches. As the sole level designer, I rebuilt the stage to reflect the mother’s reconciliation with her family, emphasizing distinct, intuitive spaces tied to each member’s psyche.
